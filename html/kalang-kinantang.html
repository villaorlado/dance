Javanese context

Kalang-kinantang are proud and strong characters. The arms are moved in asymmetrical patterns and contrasting lines and faces almost directly forward (Soedarsono, 1985, 228). Examples from Sendratari Ramayana include Rahwana (the ogre king, who is the enemy of Rama).
