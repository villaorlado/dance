Wanara means monkey. The simian characters come in many varieties and they are a subset of gagah. A Sendratari Ramayana example of a refined wanara character (such as the one in this video and data) is Hanoman. Other wanara characters include Subali, Sugriwa, Anggada and Anila. Not all of these characters are represented in Sendratari Ramayana, but the 19th century Langen Mandra Wanaran features a wide variety of monkey characters. This form is now rarely performed.
