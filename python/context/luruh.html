Luruh means "extremely refined", and it is a subtype of the halus[refined] characters. Examples of luruh characters in Sendratari Ramayana are Rama and Laksmana. When they first enter the stage, the musical accompaniment is usually in irama 2, a slightly slower rhytmic pattern than the one used for gagah characters. In wayang topeng, an example of a luruh character is Panji Asmorobangun.
